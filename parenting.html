Parenting

What are parenting styles?

Parenting styles are the typical and standard set of techniques that parents and/or guardians use in child rearing. Developmental psychologists discovered four types of parenting styles: Authoritative, Authoritarian, Permissive, and Neglectful/Uninvolved.
Researchers have long been interested in how parenting styles might affect child development. Some studies have found that there are links between parenting styles and the effects that these styles have on children. (Baumrind, 1991; Maccoby & Martin, 1983)
